Biography

Dr. Tarafdar studied BSc at Ramakrishna Mission Vidyamandira, Belur. He passed MSc at University of Calcutta having specialization in Organic Chemistry. Later, he was inclined to biological processes and pursued PhD at School of Chemistry, University of Hyderabad under the supervision of Prof. Musti J. Swamy.
